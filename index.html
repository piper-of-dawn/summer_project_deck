UNSECURED PORTFOLIOS

This is a jargon for credit cards.

Ending Net Recievable (ENR)

The amount that is owed by clients to Citi at the end of the payment cycle.


Gross Credit Losses (GCL%ENR)

The amount that is not paid on or before the billing date or deadline.


Recoveries

The part of Gross Credit Losses recovered by Citi.


Net Credit Losses (NCL%ENR)

NCL = GCL - Recovery


Deliquency Ratio

The proportion of portfolios that have delayed payments. Deliquent portfolios / Total portfolios


Deliquency Buckets

30 DPD

60 DPD

120 DPD


FICO Score

FICO, originally Fair, Isaac and Company, is a data analytics company which credit rates individuals and businesses. Its ratings are widely used in credit risk analytics.


Lifetime of an asset

If a loan is due to be paid in 10 years then the lifetime of the asset is 10 years.


Instalment Segment

When the principle is pre-determined at the beginning of the lifetime of the asset and the loan is paid in regular instalments.


Revolving Segment

When the principle itself is a random variable. Eg: Credit Cards


Rewrite Segment

When the defaulted bad loans are reconverted into another asset.

Model Validation

The fundamental objective of Risk Assessment is making predictions. Predictions are data driven. Generally large banks use models made out of every prediction algo ever discovered. Eg: There are models based on Regression, autoregressive moving averages, Machine learning, dynamic ML, deep learning etc etc.... No matter what kind of model it is, it has to go through a rigorous validation procedure before it is used for predictive analytics.

A short overview of testing procedure

Stage 1 of model testing

GP1: Current state of portfolio

GP2: Similarity between current state and the Go forward state of portfolio

GP3: Senstivity to training values

GP4:

GP5: Segmentation and Clustering

Stage 2 of model testing

Stress testing

Model is given data shocks which are stress values and we see how model performs in such a scenario

Stage 3 of model testing

Back testing

Model predictions are tested when actual values are realised. And we examine if model is able to predict it with accuracy
